Priority shipping & Insurance:

Express shipping with immediate insurance cover when goods are confirmed lost. The delivery time is around 1 to 3 days for most metro areas. WA, NT & regional areas may require additional service time. Priority shipping orders are dispatched up to 3 times daily and in stock orders are dispatched either same day or next working day.Our warehouse working days are Monday to Friday (excluding public holidays) shipping to Australian addresses only. Please be aware that orders placed prior to the listed cut-off times are still subject to credit clearance and stock availability. When requested, additional shipping information and tracking numbers can be provided for all orders.If for some reason you do not receive your purchase within the delivery timeframe, please email support@techcart.com.au with your order number. We will follow up on your enquiry immediately. Signature on delivery is required so it is important to be at the nominated address to receive the goods. In the event that you are not present, a notification card will be left at the address so that you can arrange a re-delivery or collect from a local depot.All items should be inspected upon delivery and any damaged box should always be signed as damaged in transit. By signing for the goods indicates that you have received the shipment in good condition and no future insurance claim will be possible.
